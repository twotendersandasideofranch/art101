Making Shin Ramyun
  1. Gather a pot
  2. Add 2.5 cups of water
  3. Bring water to boil
  4. Open Shin Ramyun package
  5. Locate seasoning and dried vegetables package
  6. Add the contents of the two packages, to the pot, as the water starts to boil
  7. Add the noodles, to the pot, after the seasoning and dried vegetables are added
  8. Cook contents of the pot for 5 minutes
  9. Stir occasionally
  10. After cooking the noodles, remove from the pot
  11. Enjoy the noodles!
